Turf Kings – Tournament Rules

← Back to Home

  1. Tournament Dates: The tournament will be held on 29–30 November, 2025. Semi-final and Final will be conducted on 30 November after 6 PM.
  2. Match Fees: Rs. 2,500 to be submitted before the first match.
  3. Team Slot Confirmation: Rs. 1,000 (advance).
  4. Prizes: Winner receives Rs. 10,000 + trophy; Runner-up receives Rs. 6,000 + trophy.
  5. Awards: Overall – Winning Trophy, Runner-Up Trophy, Best Bowler, Best Batsman, Player of the Tournament. For Every Match – Best Bowler, Best Batsman, Player of the Match.
  6. Umpires: Two cameras will be placed at popping creases using tripod stands (tripods arranged by organiser). Umpire decision is final.
  7. Use of Cameras: Cameras used by umpires for close calls (run-out, no-ball, height no-ball, etc). Each team gets 2 DRS reviews per match. Same rules as international cricket.
  8. Tournament Format: 16 teams, knockout format. If fewer than 12 teams register, tournament will be cancelled and entry fees refunded.
  9. Team Requirements: Every team must have a team name.
  10. Match Format: 8 overs per inning, intended to finish within 45 minutes per match.
  11. Ball: New ball for every inning. Ball used will be SIXIT.
  12. Bowling Restrictions: In 8 overs, 3 bowlers can bowl a maximum of 2 overs each.
  13. Scoring: Matches, scores, and NRR will be monitored on Stumps App.
  14. Awards After Each Match: Player of the Match, Best Bowler, and Best Batsman awarded with medal/trophy.
  15. Conduct: Jerk not allowed.
  16. Tie Rules: In case of a tie, Super Over will decide the winner. Same rules as international cricket.
  17. Team Composition: A team consists of 7 players + 1 Impact player.
  18. Player Info: All player information must be submitted before the match.
  19. Multiple Team Restriction: A player cannot play for more than one team. If caught, the team will be disqualified.
  20. Reporting Time: Teams must report 10 minutes before their match. Late arrival results in automatic toss win for the opponent (if they arrived on time).
  21. General Rules: Basic cricket rules follow international standards. In case of any issue, organiser & umpire decision is final.
